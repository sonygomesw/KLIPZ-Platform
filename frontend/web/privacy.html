Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account information (name, email, username)
  • Social media account data (TikTok, Twitch, YouTube)
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Content submissions and platform activity

2. Social Media Integration

When you connect your social media accounts:

  • TikTok: We access basic profile information, follower count, video count, and likes for platform functionality
  • Twitch: We access profile information and follower data
  • YouTube: We access channel information and subscriber data

We do not post content on your behalf or access private messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the KLIPZ platform
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Connect streamers with content creators
  • Display user statistics and performance metrics
  • Send platform notifications and updates
  • Improve our services and user experience

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information:

  • With payment processors (Stripe) for transaction processing
  • With social media platforms for account integration
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your consent for specific purposes

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

6. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Disconnect social media accounts
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve platform functionality and user experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Social Media APIs: Account integration

These services have their own privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate data protection measures.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.

13. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@klipz.cc
